About Me
My name is Jeremy Shaw, and photography has been my passion since I was little and drained my mother's iPhone 3 battery taking pictures everywhere we went. I studied at the University of California (Los Angeles), where I graduated with a degree in fine arts. I mostly do freelance work, since I have trouble holding down other jobs. Web design is a new hobby of mine, so forgive my site for its lackluster appearance.
